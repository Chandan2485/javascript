Take two numbers a,b and only print the decimal part of the result obtained when a is divided by b

Input:
Two numbers - a, b
Output:
Decimal part (with a precision of 2 digits) of the quotient obtained when a is divided by b.

Example:

Input:
23
4
Output:
0.75

Explanation:
a = 23, b = 4;
a/b = 5.75
Decimal part = 0.75
